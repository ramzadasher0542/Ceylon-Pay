### ✅ POS CHECKOUT INTEGRATION

**What Happens on F9 (Cash Payment):**

1. **Validate** cart not empty
2. **Confirm** total amount
3. **Create** order record
4. **Add** order items
5. **Update** product stock (DEDUCT quantities)
6. **Add** payment record
7. **Log** SALE to audit_logs
8. **Kick** cash drawer (console log for now)
9. **Log** DRAWER_KICK to audit_logs
10. **Clear** cart
11. **Show** success message

**Full Traceability:**
- Know WHO made the sale (currentUser)
- Know WHAT was sold (order_items)
- Know WHEN it happened (timestamp)
- Know HOW MUCH (total_cents)
- Track STOCK changes (before/after)
